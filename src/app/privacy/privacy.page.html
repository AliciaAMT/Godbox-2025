You can also opt-out from being tracked by Google Analytics with effect for the future by downloading and installing Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Addon for your current web browser: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en.

Although these regulations only apply specifically to users from within the European Union, this website will extend the same protections to all users on this website. Under these regulations, your rights include:

  • The right to understand what information is being collected
